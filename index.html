Our Story

About Kasaudhan Collection

A trusted local fashion store serving Gadakul, Shohratgarh, and Siddharthnagar for over 5 years.

Kasaudhan Collection is a family-owned clothing store founded by Anoop Kasaudhan with a passion for bringing the latest fashion trends to every household at affordable prices. We specialize in stylish and comfortable clothing for men and kids.

Our store stocks a wide variety of products including premium denim jeans, casual and formal shirts, graphic T-shirts, kids wear, track lowers, leather belts, fashion slippers, and seasonal winter wear. Quality, comfort, and affordability are our core values.
